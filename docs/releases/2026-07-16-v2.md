## Known frayed ends (carried into vX, not shipped)

These were surfaced by the 2026-07-16 reconciliation audit and are tracked to completion
in the vX ledger (R1–R12) — listed here so the release record is honest about what is
*not* yet wired:

- **K1** — durable interpreter hardcodes `faithfulUnion`; a durable run of a moded op
  silently degrades. Reconcile-mode dispatch wiring pending. (R4)
- **K2** — Loop-3 autofix rung is dead at the caller level org-wide; `ci.yml` job-chain
  not yet adopted in `sux`/`suxrouter`/`suxlib`. (R5)
- **K4** — `sux-fileops` retired in docs but still in `managed-repos.json` and building.
  De-registration pending; archive is owner-gated. (R6)

## Notes

- **Auth:** all Claude workflows, including the required `security-review` gate, run on the
  `CLAUDE_CODE_OAUTH_TOKEN` subscription pool (consolidated by decision). Accepted risk +
  revert path documented in `.github/CLAUDE.md`.
- **Next arc is gated, not scheduled.** No v3 capability work starts until the fabric DoD
  gate passes (backlog drains to zero unattended for 7 consecutive days, spine green). The
  arc itself is then chosen by the §4 decision table in the vX doc — a reading of
  `fabric-status.json`, not a re-litigation.
